Overview

The CUJ Student App is a broader student product experience that brings together information, services, and support into one mobile interface. Rather than scattering functions across disconnected pages or channels, the app centralizes what students need in a cleaner and more accessible system.

  • The AI support flow helps students navigate questions faster inside a familiar interface.
  • Campus utilities and notifications are framed as one joined experience instead of separate tools.
  • The design direction prioritizes clarity and trust so the app feels dependable for daily use.
