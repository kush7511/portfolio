Overview

The Driver EV App is the operational companion to the broader tracking system. Its job is to simplify the driver experience by making requests, locations, and route actions easier to process in the moment.

  • The driver UI is centered on fast comprehension so the next action is always clear.
  • Pickup requests and map context are presented together instead of split across separate channels.
  • The system supports a more professional and organized transport operation for drivers on campus.
