Overview

The EV Tracking System is designed to make campus transportation more visible and easier to coordinate. Instead of relying on fragmented manual communication, the platform creates a live loop between students requesting pickups and drivers responding through a structured routing interface.

  • Student-side request flow improves transparency around ride status and expected movement.
  • Driver visibility helps operators act faster by seeing location context and incoming requests.
  • The experience is built around a campus transport use case where timing and clarity matter every day.
